    WIZ SAYS:

    When Business Transformation  meets Analytics 
    This is where we work. Our Management Consultants,  “Insight Coaches” and Data Architects work with business leaders to:

    INFORM

    We work with leadership teams to brief them on what sector best practice looks like - and to equip them to make strategic choices on the risks and benefits of deploying analytics in their own business for competitive edge.

    ADVISE

    We work with managers across multiple functions to identify and scope opportunities (or indeed challenges) where analytics could make a difference - with a particular focus on the non-technical obstacles (organisational, people-related, skills, etc)

    TRANSFORM

    We design and architect analytic solutions to transform business - as pilot or production projects (often working with your own technical staff). We also provide business-savvy interim managers (full-time) or "Data Mentors" (a few days a month) toget things started before you hire a full time expert, to strengthen your team or to recover failing projects
